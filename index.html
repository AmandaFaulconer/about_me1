Welcome to my page. My name is Amanda Faulconer. I’m originally from Seattle, Washington. I moved to Whitefish 9 years ago. I am going to school for my bachelors or possibly my master’s degree in computer science. I have two dogs. Their names are Paco and Rincon. I enjoy taking them on walks or to the dog park in Whitefish on nice days. During the Winter, I like to snowboard and ski on Whitefish Mountain Resort.

My favorite seasons are Fall and Winter. I do not have much love for Summer. I don’t like it when the weather gets too hot. I work as a Hotel Manager, so I don’t get a lot of free time. When I do, I enjoy going out in Whitefish. I like to go watch live music, comedy nights or go to trivia night. As for music, I listen to a lot of rap, hip-hop and R&B. I probably drink way to much coffee. At night, I love ending my day with a glass of red wine.
