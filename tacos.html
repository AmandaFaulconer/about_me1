Chili Lime Chicken Tacos with Grilled Pinapple Salsa

Make the marinate for the chili lime chicken. In a bowl mix olive oil, chili powder, chipotle chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, pepper, brown sugar, lime juice, and lime zest. Make your grilled pineapple salsa. Grease grill and set to med/high heat. Drizzle red onions, pineapple, bell peppers and jalapenos with olive oil and thread onto skewers.

Turn each occasionally. Allow to cool and dab off excess oil. Chop up each item into small bits. Place all in a bowl and toss with lime juice, ginger, cumin, salt and pepper, then chill. The avocado crema. Mix avocados, sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, salt, cumin and garlic powder in a food processor until smooth. Grease and heat the grill to medium heat. Grill the chicken breasts for 5-7 minutes per side, undisturbed, until it’s cooked all the way through. Assemble tacos on corn tortillas.
